What is WineRy?

  • An app that allows you to select the best wine for your party!
  • Decide whats more important for you: Ratings?, Price?, APK?
  • Are you interested in purchasing your bottle based on the Country of origin?
  • Are you looking for a particular Variety of wine?

WineRy will help you with that and much more!

Dataset - Our sources

  • Wine ratings and descriptions were extracted from tidytuesday repo
  • Details on prices and availibility were available at System Bolaget and Vinmonopolet APIs.
